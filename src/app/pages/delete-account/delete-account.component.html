How to Delete Your Account 2

If you wish to delete your Festiva account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Festiva app on your mobile device.
  2. Go to your Profile tab.
  3. Tap on Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account at the bottom of the screen.
  5. Confirm your decision. Your account and personal data will be permanently deleted.

If you encounter any issues, feel free to contact us at info@festiva.no.
